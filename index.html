About Me

"Good Design follows all the rules and makes the viewer feel comfortable. Great Design sets new rules, and makes the viewer change the way they think about something"

Born and raised in Bellville South, my work reflects the streets where I was born. In my younger days, I was obsessed with cartoons and graffiti culture, this is where my love for drawing came from. Over the years I have adapted my skills to the graphic design industry, creating cool and fresh design for any type of business. All my work starts from scratch, I like using other peoples work for reference but I try to do something new with each design.
